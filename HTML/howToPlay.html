How to play

Commands

Use the directional keys (Up, Right, Left Down) to move and use the spacebar to shoot.
If you get hit once, it's Game Over!

Classic mode

Play a classic game of Space Invaders!
Try to get the maximum amount of points

Asteroid Rain

Defend yourself from a rain of asteroids!
The asteroids will move faster as time goes on

Boss fight

Fight against a formidable boss!
Defeat the boss by hitting it 50 times while avoiding its minions and its shots
